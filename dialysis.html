Dialysis Center

Our state-of-the-art unit offers hemodialysis, peritoneal dialysis, and home therapies under expert supervision.

Our Dialysis Services

At Al‑Saegh Specialty Center, we offer a full spectrum of dialysis modalities to accommodate each patient's unique medical needs. Our multidisciplinary team ensures safe, effective treatments in a compassionate environment.

Hemodialysis

Our hemodialysis unit is equipped with advanced machines and staffed by experienced nurses. Patients receive therapy in comfortable, private stations with continuous monitoring.

Peritoneal Dialysis

We provide training and support for patients performing dialysis at home using the peritoneal membrane. Our team ensures safe technique and ongoing follow-up.

Home Dialysis

For eligible patients, we offer home hemodialysis programmes. This allows greater flexibility and independence while maintaining excellent outcomes.
